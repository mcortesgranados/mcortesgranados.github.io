Company Experience 4

📌 Name of Company: PORVENIR Pensiones y Cesantías S.A.

💼 Role: PL/SQL Developer

📜 Project Name: Sistema de Información Comercial

📍 Duration: June 2002 – March 2003 (9 months)

🛠️ Technologies Used:

  • PL/SQL Developer (SQL, PL/SQL, Web PL/SQL)
  • Oracle Designer (Design Editor, Database Transformer Editor, etc.)
  • Oracle JDeveloper
  • Macromedia DreamWeaver (HTML, Java, JavaScript)

🎯 Key Contributions & Achievements:

✅ Participated in the design, analysis, programming, testing, and maintenance of the Sistema de Información Comercial, aimed at supporting the Commercial Department and its processes.

✅ Developed two core modules of the system in collaboration with a team of two other developers.

✅ Designed and implemented 480 PCK files, each containing approximately 5 stored procedures/functions, significantly improving the system’s data processing capabilities.

✅ Utilized PL/SQL and Web PL/SQL to optimize commercial data workflows and enhance the performance of sales and customer-related operations.

✅ Developed interactive front-end components using Macromedia DreamWeaver, Java, and JavaScript, ensuring user-friendly interfaces for commercial staff.

🛠️ Technical Skills Acquired:

🔹 PL/SQL Programming & Web PL/SQL Development
🔹 Oracle Database Development & Optimization
🔹 Stored Procedures, Functions & Packages Creation
🔹 Oracle Designer for Database Modeling & System Design
🔹 Web Development (HTML, JavaScript) for Commercial Applications

📚 Lessons Learned & Impact:

✔️ Strengthened expertise in PL/SQL database development and Oracle Designer tools.
✔️ Gained hands-on experience in database-driven web applications.
✔️ Contributed to the successful modernization of commercial operations through database-driven automation.
✔️ Improved team collaboration and full software development lifecycle (SDLC) understanding.
