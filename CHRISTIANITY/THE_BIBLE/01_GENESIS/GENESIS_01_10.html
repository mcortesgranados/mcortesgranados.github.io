🌍 Genealogy of Noah’s Sons:

1. Descendants of Jafet 🌊:

  • Jafet's children:

    • Gómer, Magog, Madai, Javán, Tubal, Mésec, and Tirás
      🏖️ Settlements: These descendants populated coastal regions. Each nation developed its own language and culture.

2. Descendants of Cam 🔥:

  • Cam's children:

    • Cus, Misraim, Fut, and Canaán
      🌍 Cus' descendants include famous figures like Nimrod, the great hunter and ruler of Babel.
      🏰 Cities Built: Nimrod founded several cities such as Babel, Érec, and Nínive.
      Misraim's descendants became the Philistines.
      Canaán's descendants settled in a vast region that extended from Sidón to Gaza, and their cities included the notorious Sodom and Gomorra.

3. Descendants of Sem 🌱:

  • Sem's children:

    • Elam, Asur, Arfaxad, Lud, and Aram
      🌾 Notable Descendants: Arfaxad’s descendants, particularly Éber, gave rise to the Hebrews, through his sons Péleg (named for the division of the earth) and Joctán.
      🌍 Settlements: The descendants of Joctán inhabited lands from Mesá to Sefar.

🌟 Conclusion:

The descendants of Noah’s sons, Jafet, Cam, and Sem, spread across the earth after the flood, forming distinct nations, cultures, and languages. 🌍 Each group left a significant mark on history, whether through the coastal regions of Jafet or the mighty cities built by Nimrod’s descendants in Cam’s line. 🌱 Sem’s descendants notably contributed to the Hebrew people, who would go on to play a major role in biblical history. 📖
